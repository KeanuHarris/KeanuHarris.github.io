The Twelve Kizuki

The Upper Moons are the strongest demons under Muzan’s command, ranked from One to Six.

  1. Kokushibo – Upper Moon One
  2. Doma – Upper Moon Two
  3. Akaza – Upper Moon Three
  4. Hantengu – Upper Moon Four
  5. Gyokko – Upper Moon Five
  6. Kaigaku – Upper Moon Six
